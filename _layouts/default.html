From: Lorenzo Capicchioni

Born: 1989

Location: Italy

To you reading this,

The dizzy feeling when you open your eyes after a nap outside, and the sun is shining directly into your eyes.

For a moment everything melts into everything until consciousness returns.

This is the best way to describe what I do. The way I paint pretty much reflects this awakening.

It would be easy to say "art is a call." I say "art is a choice." Just like love is a choice.

It's hard attending a school of art, and it's hard being self-taught. I am the latter kind. It's hard being an artist anyway.

There is no recipe; there is a process. You have to trust the process.

You have to believe that, whatever it takes, you have it. And when you don't have it, it's simply unfinished.

Failure is my ultimate friend. Oil painting is about allowing myself to make mistakes.

To you reading these words, thank you. You and I are a byproduct of time.

Every painting on this website is also a matter of time.

Time is our enemy.

There is beauty in things that don't happen. Every painting I make is in search of this same beauty.
